hi im flo. built my first website at 12 — a fanpage for my favourite football club in microsoft frontpage. uploading it to an ftp server felt like magic. sold my first site at 14, to a dj. designed in photoshop, coded in html+css, with a flash player for the songs. peak 2000s.


      
after that came my maths teacher's art portfolio and a full rebuild of my school's website over a weekend. fell in love with design through the PSP homebrew scene — psp-source had a GFX section where kids with cracked photoshop made tiny artworks called "signatures" and taught each other the basics. that's where it started for me.


      
studied communication design, been in the game for 10+ years now. today i specialise in design systems — the infrastructure that lets teams ship without chaos. full story on linkedin. for the serious work, see gampert.io.
